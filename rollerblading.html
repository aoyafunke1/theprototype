My Start - Why I skate

Over a decade ago, a buddy and I were meeting up. I arrived, getting off the
dreary bus with what felt like a thousand people who had lives too important
for them to wait calmly while we all filtered out gracefully.

Following the sigh of relief, followed quickly by the taste of fresh, unused
air, I look up to catch a whirling noise I can hear in the distance - who should
it be but the buddy of mine I was meeting up with!

He came rocketing down toward me - his back facing me as his body charged
in my direction with no indication of slowly! He was... skating backwards! (video
